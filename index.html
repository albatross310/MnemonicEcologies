DotComma

A constrained-language game and online community for writing in short, plain words. A bridge language — an exercise in radical clarity that draws new words from languages around the world, building an ecology of borrowed expression.

dotcomma.com.au →

Live

Global Threat Forum

An archival website and moderated forum for thinking creatively about existential risk beyond climate change. Seeks to create permanent conversation snapshots rooted in moments in time — a living record of how humanity thought about its biggest threats.

globalthreatforum.com →

In development

Inktrain

A board game where you build words out of trains of words — each word's last letters must match the next word's first letters. 676 mnemonic picture tiles (26×26 combinations) rendered in woodcut, single-colour shading, and oil paint.
